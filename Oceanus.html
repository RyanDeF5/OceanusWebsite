The Oceanus Promise, From Our Founder:
Albert Büchi was a Swiss engineer who is credited with inventing the turbocharger. In the early 20th century, he developed the first successful turbocharging system for diesel engines, with his 1905 patent marking a major milestone. Büchi's design used exhaust gases to drive a turbine that compressed intake air, significantly improving engine efficiency and power output. His innovation laid the foundation for modern turbocharged engines used in everything from trucks and ships to performance cars and airplanes.
